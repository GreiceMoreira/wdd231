Beira-Rio Stadium

Av. Padre Cacique, 891 - Praia de Belas, Porto Alegre

Home of Sport Club Internacional, this iconic stadium hosted matches during the 2014 FIFA World Cup and offers guided tours for football fans.

Redenção Park (Farroupilha Park)

Av. João Pessoa, s/n - Farroupilha

A popular green space in the city, perfect for walks, picnics, and cultural events, with a famous Sunday flea market.

Gasometer Power Plant

Av. Pres. João Goulart, 551 - Centro Histórico

An old power plant transformed into a cultural space, offering exhibitions and one of the best sunset views by the Guaíba River.

Mario Quintana House of Culture

R. dos Andradas, 736 - Centro Histórico

A vibrant cultural center housed in a historic hotel, featuring art exhibitions, theaters, and tributes to the poet Mário Quintana.

Iberê Camargo Foundation

Av. Padre Cacique, 2000

An architectural landmark by Álvaro Siza, this museum showcases the works of Iberê Camargo and contemporary art exhibitions.

Moinhos de Vento Park (Windmills Park)

R. Comendador Caminha, s/n - Moinhos de Vento

A charming park in one of Porto Alegre’s most upscale neighborhoods, featuring jogging paths, a pond, and a replica of an Azorean windmill.

São Pedro Theater

Praça Marechal Deodoro, s/n - Centro Histórico

A beautifully restored 19th-century theater, hosting plays, concerts, and cultural performances in an elegant setting.

Botanical Garden

R. Dr. Salvador França, 1427 - Jardim Botânico

One of the most important botanical gardens in Brazil, preserving native plant species and offering a peaceful retreat in the city.
