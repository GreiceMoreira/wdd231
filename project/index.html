About the Group

The Granny Square Club is a warm and welcoming online space where crochet lovers of all levels come together to learn, share, and create. Whether you're just picking up a crochet hook for the first time or you're looking to refine your skills, this community is for you!

Learn with Kelly Franceschetti

Led by experienced crochet instructor Kelly Franceschetti, our lessons cover everything from beginner stitches to advanced techniques.Through interactive tutorials, live workshops, and an encouraging group environment, you'll gain confidence in your crochet skills, and create stunning granny squares along the way.
