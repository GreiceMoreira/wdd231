Rio Grande do Sul

Rio Grande do Sul ("Great River of the South") is a state in the southern region of Brazil. It is the fifth-most populous state and the ninth-largest by area.
The state shares a gaucho culture with its neighbors Argentina and Uruguay. Large waves of German and Italian migration have shaped the state as well.
